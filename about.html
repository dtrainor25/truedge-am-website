About TruEdge

Built by practitioners.

Over 150 years of combined industry expertise, united by a single mission: making independent advisors more successful.

Who We Are

Dependable by design,
consistent by commitment

TruEdge Asset Management is a federally registered investment adviser built from the ground up to serve independent financial advisors. We were founded on the belief that every advisor — regardless of firm size — deserves institutional-grade tools, technology, and operational support.

Our team consists of hands-on practitioners with deep expertise in advisory technology, compliance, middle and back office operations, and business consulting. We don't just understand the advisory business — we've lived it.

Our Mission

The most advisor-centric
TAMP in the industry

We exist to empower independent advisors with the wealth management tools, technology, and resources they need to succeed — so they can focus on what matters most: their clients.

Our vision is to become the most advisor-centric TAMP in the industry by continuously innovating and adapting our platform to meet the evolving needs of independent practices of every size.

Our Core Values

The pillars that guide everything we do

Robust Infrastructure

We build and maintain customizable, scalable solutions that provide reliable back-office efficiency for independent advisors of every size.

Scalable Growth

Our personalized support model is designed to be accessible, efficient, and cost-effective — so advisors can grow without growing their overhead.

Advisor-Centric Approach

Everything we build is designed with one person in mind — the independent advisor. Your success is the only metric that matters to us.
