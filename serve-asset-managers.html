Asset Managers

A direct line to the independent RIA market

Reaching independent RIAs through traditional distribution channels is costly, time-consuming, and increasingly inefficient. TruEdge's model marketplace offers asset managers a better way to connect their strategies with advisors who are actively looking for institutional-quality investment solutions.

By listing your model portfolios on the TruEdge platform, you gain access to a growing network of independent advisors who are empowered to implement your strategies directly in their clients' accounts.

Direct RIA market access

Seamless model integration

Due diligence credibility

Scalable distribution

How It Works

From strategy submission to advisor implementation

Getting your strategies in front of TruEdge advisors is a straightforward process designed to minimize friction on both sides.

1
Strategy Submission & Due Diligence

Submit your model portfolio for review. TruEdge's investment team evaluates process, risk management, performance history, and organizational stability.

2
Platform Integration

Approved strategies are integrated into the TruEdge model marketplace and made available to advisors on the platform.

3
Advisor Discovery & Selection

TruEdge advisors browse the marketplace, review strategy details, and select models that align with their clients' investment objectives.

4
Ongoing Reporting & Updates

Provide regular updates to the TruEdge team. Continued presence in the marketplace is supported by ongoing communication and performance reporting.
